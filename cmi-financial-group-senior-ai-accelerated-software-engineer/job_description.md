## Full job description

Senior AI-Accelerated Software Engineer Opening

Help shape the future of private lending in Canada by modernizing the systems behind real, end-to-end mortgage operations—where your decisions directly affect daily workflows, data integrity, and operational reliability.

At CMI, we are intentionally moving toward AI-accelerated delivery. Senior engineers here own problem framing, solution design, and correctness, while AI tools are used to accelerate analysis, implementation, refactoring, and investigation.

This role is for senior engineers who built systems the traditional way, and now deliver faster and more effectively by working with AI—without giving up judgment, accountability, or quality.

### About CMI

CMI Financial Group is an award-winning leader in the Canadian non-bank financial services market.

Our companies work collectively to originate, underwrite, fund, and service private mortgages end-to-end for mortgage brokers and investors.

Our mission is to modernize the private market by delivering innovative products and exceptional service—while preserving the reliability and trust required in financial systems.

### The Role & How You'll Deliver

This is a senior, hands-on engineering role focused on AI-accelerated delivery with ownership across discovery, solution shaping, and production delivery.

You will spend less time manually typing boilerplate and more time defining intent, constraints, and correctness—using AI to accelerate understanding and execution while remaining accountable for outcomes.

You will:

- Partner with stakeholders to transform loosely defined business or operational problems into clear, implementable scopes using AI-assisted discovery and synthesis, and build tools that enable stakeholders to better define and structure these problems independently.
- Use AI tools (Copilot, Claude, Cursor, or equivalent) as a primary development workflow to accelerate implementation, refactoring, debugging, testing, and documentation.
- Define and socialize a practical AI-assisted engineering workflow that improves delivery speed while keeping code review, testing, security, and maintainability at production standards.
- Establish team guardrails for AI usage, including when to use AI, what must be verified, what requires human judgment, and how to document intent, constraints, and decisions.
- Review, adapt, and harden AI-generated output to meet production standards for maintainability, performance, security, and long-term system health.
- Design and maintain APIs and integration points that connect internal systems and external partners, reducing manual workflow friction.
- Work across front-end experiences, backend services, and integration points to deliver end-to-end behavior.
- Contribute to modernization initiatives and incremental improvement of legacy systems using AI-assisted analysis.
- Act as an AI-accelerated delivery champion by modeling responsible AI usage and sharing patterns and guardrails.
- Bring an outgoing, positive, and enabling presence to collaboration, helping stakeholders think more clearly without creating friction.
